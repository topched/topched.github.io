Welcome to Floor Coat Inc.

Floor Coat Inc. is an Edmonton based decorative flooring company serving all Alberta. We specialize in concrete restoration, offering a wide range of flooring options including: polyaspartic garage & commercial floor coatings, decorative epoxy, grind & polished concrete, surface preparation, and many other coatings ideal for any interior or exterior surfaces.

What sets us aside from everyone else? We are a family run business who takes great pride in our work so you can trust that you are getting the best in every job. We are craftsmen who have combined over 60 years of residential or commercial contracting experience. We are real professionals who have many unique pieces of equipment helping us conquer any job. We build trust and great relationships with our clients. We have tested many different products in numerous applications so we know what flooring option will work best for you.
