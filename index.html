Welcome To Floor Coat

Floor Coat is an Edmonton-based decorative flooring company serving all of Alberta. We offer a wide range of flooring options and specialize in concrete resurfacing and restoration. Some of our services include: polyaspartic garage & commercial floor coatings, grind & polished concrete, surface preparation, decorative epoxy and many other coatings for any interior or exterior surface.

What sets Floor Coat apart from everyone else? We build trust and great relationships with our clients. We are a family run business that takes great pride in our work. We are craftsmen who have over 70 years combined of residential and commercial contracting experience. We are a team of innovative professionals. We have extensive product knowledge and are experts at what we do, giving us complete confidence to guarantee our work to you.
