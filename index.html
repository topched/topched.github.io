Polyaspartic Chip & Quartz Floor Coating

Floor Coat Inc. specializes in polyaspartic floor coatings with excellent wear capabilities to coordinate your floors and walls with our extensive range of styles. We offer a wide selection of Polyaspartic flooring systems including Full Broadcast Polyaspartic Chip system is the most durable garage floor protection system on the market. That’s not all, we have coatings for every area of your building: Quartz flooring for locker rooms, up the walls around toilets and showers; easy cleaning heavy-duty chemical & Oil resistant finishes; abrasion resistant systems. Our in-depth attention to detail for preparation includes filling cracks and other patching followed by a diamond grind on the floor sets up for success and the best product adhesion. From a simple garage floor chip coating to the perfect floor of the ultimate man cave, we do it all. We offer design, experience and guaranteed products giving you the reassurance you’re getting the right people for the right job.
