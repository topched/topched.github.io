Commercial Flooring
  • Product Info
  • Floor Coat Inc. offers decorative flooring solutions for all types of commercial floors. We can add a unique design to a showroom floor, resurface damaged or dangerous concrete, or provide safety with a non-slip coating. Some of our commercial flooring options include grind & polished concrete, polyaspartic & epoxy floor coatings, exterior concrete stair coatings, surface preparation and custom logos.
  • What We Do
  • If you are looking for the ultimate commercial floor, polished concrete is very popular because of its exceptional look and durability in high-traffic areas. Other commercial flooring options include polyaspartic or epoxy with quartz, which combine for a high-performance multi-functional floor that’s decorative, sanitary, and slip-resistant. We can save you money, in most cases there’s no need to replace old concrete we offer experience in major concrete restoration.
  • Benefits
  • Safety: Add traction without compromising appearance. We provide many non-slip coating options for all types of interior or exterior concrete.
  • Brand Recognition: Get recognized by your clients with a unique looking floor accompanied with a design or logo.
  • Easy to clean, seamless surface: Our grind and polished, polyaspartics, or epoxy floor coatings offer durable, seamless surfaces that can easily be wiped clean with very little upkeep.
  • Cost Savings: Don’t rip out and replace old concrete we can restore it and provide a durable surface that will last for a long time with easy maintenance.
  • Chemical Resistant: Our floor coatings are chemically resistant and will not be compromised by any spills making it ideal for all commercial or industrial flooring needs.
  • Typical Applications
  • Commercial Spaces, Condo Buildings, Offices, Manufacturing Plants, Show Rooms, Warehouses & Distribution Facilities, Schools, Hospitals, Retail Space, Stairs, Sidewalks, Ramps, Parkades Interior or Exterior, High Traffic Areas.
