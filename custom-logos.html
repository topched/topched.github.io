Custom Logos
  • Product Info
  • Floor Coat Inc. is the only company in Edmonton that has the capability to provide any design or logo in any type of concrete surface. We offer many types of flooring options for interior or exterior concrete and we have perfected the art of placing logos into the finished product.
  • What We Do
  • We offer all types of logos including, vinyl logos in a polyaspartic or epoxy coated floor. We can produce logos into a polished concrete floor with stencils and dye. We also have the equipment to artistically engrave logos directly into concrete. Some of our recent engraving projects include an exterior concrete engraving at the LRT entrance for the new downtown arena and also logos for the city of Edmonton and St. Albert on some of their sidewalks.
  • Benefits
  • Brand Recognition: Businesses spend money on signage all the time why not put any logo directly in one of our many flooring options.
  • Easy to clean, seamless surface: Our logos combined with our floor coatings or polished concrete offer durable surfaces that can easily be wiped clean with no maintenance.
  • Engraved into Concrete: We have specialized tools that engrave logos or designs into concrete creating a permanent unique look.
  • Typical Applications
  • Show Rooms, Retail Space, Commercial Facilities, Public Buildings, Schools, Personal Garages, Condo Buildings, Engraving Design on Urban Concrete.
