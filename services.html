  • Less maintenance and longer service life: Polished floors are easy to clean, requiring only the occasional damp mopping. Polishing eliminates the need for messy waxes or coatings.

Types Of Polished Concrete

  • Salt & Pepper Polish: This is the most common diamond polished concrete floor out there. Typically this floor finish will have some small rock exposed, but the main focus is a consistent look with polishing as the emphasis.
  • Full Aggregate Exposure: The look when we are finished is incredible, because you get a glass like flat floor with all of these colourful rocks exposed throughout. There is a considerable amount of time required to get this type of finish because of the amount of passes necessary to expose the aggregate, however, the end result is something beautiful.
