Grind & Polished Concrete
  • Product Info
  • Polished concrete is fast becoming the ultimate flooring option for high traffic commercial areas. With superb durability and performance, more retail, warehouse, and public facilities are opting for polished concrete as an alternative to marble, granite, or tile. Our polishing equipment and techniques allow us to grind and polish up any new or old concrete floors. The result is a long lasting and low maintenance floor that will save money in the long run.
  • What We Do
  • Polishing concrete is a process that involves a variety of large and small machines progressively grinding and polishing concrete to achieve the desired look. The equipment has progressive grits of diamond grinding blades followed by a series of polishing pads. The floor is then treated with a chemical densifier helping plug and strengthen the concrete. For the finishing touch a sealer is applied with a high speed burnishing pad protect your polished concrete floor.
  • Benefits
  • Cost Savings: Save money with no building materials. Repairing and re-finishing the existing slab makes this a cost effective choice for any concrete project.
  • Easy to Clean: Unlike tiles and carpet there are no grout joints or fibers to attract dust and bacteria.
  • Less maintenance and longer service life: Polished floors are easy to clean and always look good. Eliminate the need for messy waxes that tend to wear off and need to be reapplied.
  • High light reflectivity: Polished concrete presents a bright, clean, and professional image. You can also save energy by reducing artificial lighting requirements.
  • Typical Applications
  • Commercial Buildings, Schools, Hospitals, Public Facilities, Offices, Warehouses & Distribution Facilities, Show Rooms, Retail Space, Malls, High Traffic Areas, Large Area Interior Concrete Residential or Commercial.
