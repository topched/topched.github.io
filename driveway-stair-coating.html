  • What We Do
  • We offer a variety of exterior coatings for driveways and stairs, durable enough to stand up to high traffic and all types of Alberta weather. Our process involves a diamond machine grind and acid wash to prepare the concrete for the best product adhesion. We have years of concrete experience, we identify and fill cracks with special flexible caulking and fix other problematic concrete by patching with polymer reinforced materials. Once the preparation is completed multiple applications of the coating is applied. With fast curing time the finished product is available to drive on a couple days later.
  • Benefits
  • Functionality: Our concrete restoration process can turn damaged concrete into a great looking and durable non-slip surface.
  • Cost Savings: Whether resurfacing old concrete or protecting new concrete, we provide you with a hard wearing surface helping your concrete last for years with easy maintenance.
  • Salt & Chemically Resistant: Our exterior coatings are chemically resistant and salt resistant.
  • Add Life to Concrete: Replacing concrete is expensive. One of our coatings can extend the life of your concrete making it look great for a longer period of time.
